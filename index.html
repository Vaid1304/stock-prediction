📌 About the Project

The Advanced Stock Movement Prediction System uses machine learning algorithms to forecast the next-day trading direction of any publicly listed stock. By analyzing historical price data, volume patterns, and technical indicators, a Random Forest Classifier predicts whether a stock will move UP, DOWN, or remain SIDEWAYS.

The model is trained on years of historical market data obtained via Yahoo Finance (yfinance). Features such as daily returns, 5-day moving averages, and volume ratios are engineered to capture both short-term momentum and trading activity. The time-based train/test split ensures the evaluation reflects real-world conditions.
